Whitehouse Petition Responses

I got tired of scrolling through the oddly sorted response page on the Whitehouse 'We The People' petition site. So I decided to build this simple app to scrape their response page nightly, extract any new responses and display them using a dead-simple interface. If you want to view the original responses scroll to the bottom and click the button to visit the official response page on Whitehouse.gov
